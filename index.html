NakamaList is a MyAnimeList client I built because I wanted one that felt right on Android. Sign in with your MAL account and pick up where you left off, in 56 languages.

Not affiliated with MyAnimeList. Every sign-in, read and write goes through the official MyAnimeList API, with your own account.

Built for people who actually keep a list.

Most trackers stop at your list. NakamaList carries the parts of MAL that usually get left behind on mobile.

Your list, down to the last detail

Status, score, episode and chapter progress, volumes, start and finish dates, rewatch and reread counts, priority, tags and comments. Tap +1 to bump progress and watch it update on the spot.

A Home that knows you

Continue Watching and Reading, fresh episodes, plan-to picks, and what is airing this season.

Discover, every angle

All official rankings, Top Ranked, suggestions for you, full-screen search, and a random pick.

Detail pages worth reading

Synopsis, score and rank, related and recommended, characters and staff with their own pages, the trailer, theme songs and discussions.

Stats that feel like yours

Full anime and manga statistics plus on-device analytics, down to a genre-connections chart.

News, forums and clubs

The latest MAL news in an in-app reader, read-only forums with full formatting, and a clubs browser.

Notifications that matter

A nudge when a show you watch airs, or when the news touches your list, all in one bell feed.

AniList, when you want it

Connect AniList to read community reviews and keep both lists in sync, with a previewed one-way apply and automatic mirroring of your edits. Optional, and only ever your own data.

See it for yourself.

Light, dark and white themes, an accent palette, swipe navigation, and home-screen widgets.
